FAQ

Common Questions

How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping takes 5–7 business days. Express shipping (2–3 days) is available at checkout. All orders ship with full tracking and insurance.
Can I request a custom piece?
Yes! We love custom orders. Just fill out the contact form and select "Custom Jewelry Design". We'll schedule a free consultation to discuss your vision.
What is your return policy?
We offer free 30-day returns on all non-custom items. Custom pieces are non-returnable but we'll work with you to ensure you're 100% happy.
Are your gemstones ethically sourced?
Absolutely. All gemstones are conflict-free and ethically sourced, and every piece comes with a Certificate of Authenticity.
Do you offer jewelry care services?
Yes, we offer free lifetime polishing on all Zenvy pieces. Simply mail your piece back to us and we'll restore it to its original brilliance.
Which payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. We also offer 0% installment payment plans on orders over $500.
